What Unify360 Needs to Grow
Based on traffic patterns, GSC page data, AI referral signals, and CallRail behavior — these are the content assets that will move the needle.

Contractor Case Studies Highest Priority
Format: 1-page PDF + web page · 3 stories needed · Timeline: 30 days

B2B SaaS buyers need proof before they book a demo — and for home service contractors, that proof needs to look like them. A case study featuring a plumber, an HVAC company, or an electrician with specific numbers converts far better than any feature page.

ChatGPT and Gemini actively cite detailed case studies when answering "does [software] work?" questions. Three case studies would directly amplify the 88 AI sessions already arriving.

2
Interactive ROI Calculator Highest Converting
Format: Embedded web tool · Ungated · Timeline: 45 days

The most-converting B2B SaaS lead magnet is an ROI calculator — because it makes the cost of not buying feel real. For Unify360, the calculation is simple: missed calls × average job value = lost revenue that Unify360 recovers.

Place it on the homepage (1,072 clicks/month), the /review-response-software/ page (11,667 impr), and the /reputation-management-software/ page (24,565 impr). No email gate — let it run and track CTA clicks to "Book a Demo."

Calculator Inputs:
① Monthly inbound leads (calls + web forms)
② Average response time (hours)
③ Average job value ($)
④ Current review count + star rating
→ Output: "You're losing $X/month. Here's how Unify360 fixes it."
